  • Cumasc Gael || G.I.G (God Is Good) || Website Exclusive Demo
  • Cumasc Gael are proud to present this exclusive demo recording of their forthcoming single.
  • Musicians the world over often talk about 'Gigs' without giving much thought to the word itself. It's widely believed that "gig" is an acronym for "God Is Good" and was used by blues and jazz musicians in the American South to describe thier working status. (ie. I have work therefore God Is Good) Cumasc Gael's G.I.G (God Is Good) however, tells the lamentable tale of a performer who has sadly and somewhat ironically fallen out of love with his craft...
  • "...God is good when I'm heading for home!"
